Order of the O Fall 2015

The club is for alumni who have earned a varsity letter in a NCAA-accredited sport while at Oregon. The purpose of the Order of the O is to perpetuate the legacy and tradition of U of O athletics. Promoting excellence and leadership of all athletic programs by fostering enduring relationships among current athletes and former letter winners
