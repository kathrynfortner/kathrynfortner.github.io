KATHRYN FORTNER



about

20 years old, born and raised in Beaverton, OR. Currently residing in Eugene, Oregon where I am pursuing a Business Marketing degree at the University of Oregon. I am also earning minors in Spanish and Computer Information Technology. I progress with my graphic designing through my marketing internship with the U of O Athletic Department, where I assist in creating graphic advertisements for sporting events. I also give a helping hand with running promotions, social media, and customer service. When I'm not working or in class, I am either dancing on our school's club hip-hop team, or spending some quality time with friends/family.

Why Web + Graphic Design?
The beauty of creating and the limitless boundaries of technology – the main reasons I have been captivated by web and graphic design.

Our lives are digitally integrated in nearly every moment of our day, whether we're banking, shopping, texting, or listening to music – we're starring at a screen with a designed interface, some simple ... some completely awesome. The interfaces with seamlessly combined aesthetics and functionality are the ones that excite and inspire the public. I hope to be apart of this inspiration one day.
